My Illenium Fanpage

Bio

Illenium, Nick Miller, is a melodic dubstep artist who has become very popular in the past few years. His music is very emotive and touches on hard subjects like addiction, heartbreak, and finding yourself. He often collaborates with other EDM artists such as Said the Sky, Dabin, and Excision.When not on tour, he lives with his current girlfriend, Lara McWhorter, in Colorado Springs. As a fun fact, before Illenium became very popular, he produced an EP, Risen, which was comprised of heavy dubstep, and is my personal favorite.

See his official artist page here.
